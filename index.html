FAQs about our specialties

If you’re skimming options and wondering how each track works, these quick answers cover the logistics, timelines, and focus areas of our core services.

What happens inside marriage counselling?

Weekly or bi-weekly sessions follow Emotionally Focused Therapy protocols that report 70–75% recovery in outcome studies. We integrate HRV biofeedback and relapse-prevent checklists derived from Gottman Institute longitudinal research.

Is life coaching only for executives?

No. We adapt coaching frameworks validated with university and early-career samples, incorporating goal-setting theory, self-determination research, and digital accountability measures supported by randomized control trials in education settings.

How does the Goals Achieve track differ?

It combines graded exposure, motivational interviewing, and OKR planning inspired by Deloitte’s performance science labs. The structure mirrors interventions cited by the Association for Psychological Science for accelerating habit consolidation.

Do therapy sessions include families or teens?

Yes. We use attachment-based family therapy protocols with demonstrated efficacy for adolescents plus parent management training rated as gold-standard by the American Academy of Pediatrics. Caregivers receive data summaries after each block.

Can we combine in-person and telehealth?

Absolutely. Telehealth delivery follows APA telepsychology guidelines and mirrors outcomes seen in 2021 NIH-funded trials where hybrid care maintained symptom reductions comparable to in-person treatment.

How soon can we start?

Intakes open monthly. After the consent packet is submitted, we administer screening instruments (PHQ-9, GAD-7, DAS) within 12 hours and match you with a clinician whose training aligns with the presenting data.
